Subscription using subscription link

Step 1

Go to the URL: https://dealercrm.co.in/subscription/ and login using the email ID of the Service Head.

Step 2

Enter the ‘Dealer Parent Code’ and select the ‘Region’. The workshop list will be populated automatically.

Step 3

Select the workshops you wish to subscribe to and click on ‘Next’.

Step 4

  • Complete the KYC and financial details:
  • Provide all the required KYC and financial details, then click ‘Next’.
  • Review the details and click on ‘Submit’ to finalise the subscription process.
  • The workshop will be subscribed to DExC after approval by the RSM.
