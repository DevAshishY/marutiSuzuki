  • About Manual

The Maruti Suzuki Service Assurance Standards Manual is your one-stop guide to acing service procedures and customer engagement. It has been designed to simplify the complex vehicle service-related processes into clear, actionable steps. From reminder calls and appointment bookings to performing vehicle service, raising FTIRs and pitching warranties, every chapter is built to help you deliver consistent, efficient, and customer-friendly service. With practical tips, standard scripts, and process enablers, the manual ensures that you have the right tools at the right time. Easy to navigate and always accessible, the SAS manual empowers you to create a seamless, end-to-end service experience and strengthen customer relationships.
